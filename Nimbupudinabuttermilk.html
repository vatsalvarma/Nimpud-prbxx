What’s Special About This Drink?

Nimbu Pudina is a timeless refresher loved for its clean, uplifting flavor. The tang of lemon wakes up your taste buds, while mint brings a cooling, aromatic freshness that instantly feels revitalizing

This drink is light, hydrating, and perfect when you need something that refreshes without feeling heavy. Shake & Gulp Nimbu Pudina captures this classic combination in a convenient bottle — and since natural mint and lemon extracts settle, it’s meant to be shaken well before every sip.

Who Should Drink This?

Nimbu Pudina is a refreshing choice for everyone as part of daily hydration.

It’s especially helpful for people who experience:

  • Tiredness from long days outdoors
  • A heavy or sluggish feeling after meals
  • Mild dehydration
  • A need for a light, uplifting refreshment

A natural way to feel cool, light, and refreshed.
